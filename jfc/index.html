JESTIN FRIED CHIKEN

Passionately conceptualize in Thrissur, the first JFC® outlet was established in the year 1998, and since then, the brand has grown to become extremely popular in KERALA with more than 10+ outlets. In Kerala First outlet opened in Thrissur and now spread more than 10 outlets including thrissur,kochi,palakkad,kollam,etc. This impressive growth was achieved by understanding what families prefer. We are also a big favorite amongst individuals who are looking for a tasty alternative to formulaic fast food.

​

JFC, established as part of the highly reputable and renowned India based JESTIN Foods Pvt. Ltd. and RAJ Foods Pvt. Ltd. JFC® began its journey with a simple yet determined dream – To create a brand that brings happiness, satisfaction and smiles to our valued customers world-over.

Over the years, it has successfully realized this dream by making JFC® a favorite destination for real and tasty fried chicken. JFC® – A brand that has been built with over a decade of commitment and unmatched quality continues to make tremendous strides within its sphere of business. Whether its global expansion, new menu or innovative services and outlets, the change is charismatic, and the customers have been lapping it with great passion.
